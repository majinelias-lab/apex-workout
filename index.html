GRIP ROTATION — WEEKLY
Rotate grips each Pull session to prevent joint fatigue and ensure full development: Pull A → Wide pronated | Pull B → Neutral grip | Next Pull A → Chin-up supinated | Repeat. Each grip hits slightly different lat fibres and keeps joints healthy long-term.
PROGRESSION ENGINE
Three methods. Every exercise assigned to one. No ambiguity. If you know the method, you know what to do next session without thinking.
📈
DOUBLE PROGRESSION
All dumbbell exercises
Stay same weight until you hit the top of the rep range on ALL sets. Then add weight. Simple. Relentless.
    Session 1: DB Press 20kg → 8/8/7 → stay
    Session 2: DB Press 20kg → 10/9/9 → stay
    Session 3: DB Press 20kg → 12/12/11 → bump to 22.5kg
    Session 4: DB Press 22.5kg → 8/8/7 → cycle repeats
🎯
REP PR PROGRESSION
All bodyweight: pull-ups, push-ups, dips
Track total reps per session. Beat it by at least 1 rep next session. Distribution across sets doesn't matter.
    Week 1: Pull-ups 8/7/6 = 21 total
    Week 2: Pull-ups 9/7/6 = 22 total ✓
    Week 3: Pull-ups 9/8/7 = 24 total ✓
    Top-end achieved all sets → add weight or harder variation
⚡
DENSITY PROGRESSION
Abs, isolation finishers
Same reps, shorter rest. Or more reps, same time. Progress until rest hits 45s → upgrade to harder variation.
    Week 1: Hanging knee raises 3×15, 90s rest
    Week 2: Same, 75s rest ✓
    Week 3: Same, 60s rest ✓
    Week 4: Upgrade to hanging leg raises
RULES THAT PREVENT STALLING
    Tempo: 2–3 second eccentric on ALL hypertrophy lifts. Set a timer. No guessing.
    Stall: No progress 3 sessions in a row → drop weight 10%, reset, rebuild. Never grind the same weight forever.
    Deload: Every 6th week — drop weights 40%, 2 sets only. Non-negotiable for joint health and continued progress.
    Sleep: 7–9 hrs. Growth hormone peaks near 1AM. No hack replaces this.
    Protein: 1.8–2.2g/kg = 137–167g/day. Non-negotiable for hypertrophy at this bodyweight.
    Surplus: ~200 kcal/day above maintenance. Track for 2 weeks to calibrate your number.
    Lateral delts — daily mini-sets: On all non-push days, do 2×15–20 light lateral raises. 4–5x/week frequency is what makes shoulders visibly wider in 3–5 weeks.
